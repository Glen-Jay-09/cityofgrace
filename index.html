About Us


City of Grace is a spirit-filled church dedicated to transforming lives through the power of Jesus Christ. Under the leadership of Apostle Mabuyangwe (Seer 1), the ministry is committed to prayer, worship, prophetic teaching, deliverance, and the preaching of the Gospel.

At City of Grace, we believe in creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God personally, grow spiritually, and experience healing, restoration, breakthrough, and purpose. Our services are centered on the Word of God, the leading of the Holy Spirit, and building strong believers rooted in faith and holiness.

We are more than a church — we are a spiritual family united by love, worship, prayer, and a passion for seeing lives changed through Christ. Whether you are seeking spiritual growth, encouragement, deliverance, or a place to belong, City of Grace welcomes you with open arms.
